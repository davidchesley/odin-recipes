Quantum Entangled Unicorn Stew

Dish Name: Quantum Entangled Unicorn Stew Ingredients: 1 unicorn horn (ethically sourced) 3 cups of starlight (collected during a new moon) 1 bag of frozen dreams (thawed and drained) 1 pinch of non-locality (quantum field) 1 handful of cosmic dust (captured from a comet) 1 teacup of paradoxical water (collected from the event horizon of a black hole) 1 sprig of thyme (picked during a full moon) 1 tsp of time dilation (Einstein-Rosen bridge) Instructions: Begin by carefully grinding the unicorn horn to a fine powder using a mortar and pestle. In a large cauldron, combine the starlight and frozen dreams over low heat. Stir gently and wait for the mixture to come to a gentle simmer. Add the non-locality and cosmic dust to the cauldron, and continue to stir. Slowly sprinkle the powdered unicorn horn into the cauldron, stirring constantly. Allow the mixture to simmer for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally. Add the paradoxical water to the cauldron and stir vigorously. Add the thyme and time dilation to the cauldron, stirring until well combined. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for an additional 5 minutes. Remove the cauldron from the heat and let it cool for a few minutes. Serve the Quantum Entangled Unicorn Stew in a chalice made of obsidian, garnished with a sprig of thyme and a sprinkle of cosmic dust. Enjoy your surreal and mystical Quantum Entangled Unicorn Stew! Just be sure not to get too lost in the quantum field.
